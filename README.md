## Project Overview

Galaxy Cuts Barber Shop is a place where people come in for haircuts or a quick beard trim. The team’s all about helping customers walk out feeling sharp and ready to go. Sure, it’s a business, but it really has the chance to become a local favourite.
Mission:
The goal is simple: give haircuts that help people feel more confident.
Vision:
Galaxy Cuts Barber Shop wants to stand out as one of the best barber shops in the area.
Target Audience:
Most clients are men, students, and working professionals who care about their looks and like staying on trend.

## Website Goals and Objectives

The idea behind the website is growth. It should make it easier for people to find the shop and check out what it offers.
Objectives:
• Attract more customers
• Offer a quick way to view services and prices
• Highlight different hairstyle options
Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):
• More website visits
• Increased bookings and questions from customers
• More hype around Galaxy Cuts Barber Shop

## Timeline and Milestones

• Week 2: Design
• Week 3: Build
